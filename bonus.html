Carl the Chicago Chaser

Carl is a large, black, fluffy, and friendly moose from the outscurts of Chicago. Growing up he loved to watch baseball with his dad and dreamed of starting his own team, so he created the Chicago Chasers baseball team. As their mascot and creator he wanted a color that showed power and intimidation, so the team's color is black.


Fun Facts about Carl:

  • Carl has lived in Chicago for 35 years
  • He loves to read books
  • His favorite dessert in chocolate moose pie
